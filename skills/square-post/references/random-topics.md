# Random Topic Pool for Binance Square

Use this file when the user wants a random Square post topic or when scheduled Square automation needs variety.

## Rules

- Choose one topic angle that is **not** in the recent-topic list from `scripts/square_state.py recent-topics` when possible.
- Prefer rotating across different buckets instead of staying in the same theme repeatedly.
- Do not copy these phrases mechanically into the final post. Treat them as prompts, not templates.
- If a requested topic is provided by the user, follow the user instead of this pool.

- why traders overtrade chop
- protecting capital during low-conviction sessions
- waiting for confirmation instead of chasing candles
- discipline versus dopamine trading
- conviction versus noise
- when doing nothing is the best trade
- why emotional entries usually pay tuition

### Risk and execution

- stop-loss discipline in volatile markets
- why preserving capital keeps you alive for the next cycle

- high win rate versus good risk-reward
- planning exits before entries
- respecting invalidation
- capital preservation in sideways markets

### BTC-led market structure
- BTC dominance and alt patience
- why alt strength often depends on BTC stability
- clean BTC trend versus messy alt rotation
- when BTC compression can set up larger moves
- reading market mood through BTC behavior
- BTC as the risk barometer for the market

### Altcoin behavior
- fake alt breakouts versus real rotation
- why lagging alts attract emotional traders
- strength follow-through versus one-candle hype
- rotation quality in alt markets
- not every pump deserves a chase

### Sentiment and crowd behavior
- why crowded trades feel best right before pain
- social media noise versus actual edge
- euphoria, boredom, and timing
- when retail attention arrives late
- fear of missing out versus process
- how sentiment shifts faster than conviction

### Long-term mindset
- surviving long enough to catch the real trend
- consistency over hero trades
- stacking good decisions instead of forcing jackpots
- the edge of patience over months, not minutes
- sustainable trading habits in crypto
- building conviction the slow way

### Binance-native angles
- why Binance announcements can shift short-term attention fast
- launchpool attention cycles
- how listings change trader focus
- campaign/news flow versus price action
- when exchange activity fuels short-lived narratives

## Rotation suggestion

When choosing random topics repeatedly, rotate across buckets roughly like this:
1. market psychology
2. risk and execution
3. BTC-led market structure
4. altcoin behavior
5. sentiment and crowd behavior
6. long-term mindset
7. Binance-native angles

If recent topics cluster in one bucket, move to a different bucket next.
